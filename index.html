7 Wonders of the Philippines

The 7 Wonders of the Philippines are a collection of natural and cultural landmarks that showcase the country’s rich heritage and breathtaking landscapes. From ancient rice terraces carved by indigenous peoples to majestic volcanoes with near-perfect cones, these wonders reflect the Philippines' diverse geography and vibrant history. They include pristine underground rivers, surreal geological formations, marine biodiversity hotspots, and awe-inspiring natural parks. Celebrated globally, these sites are not only tourist attractions but also symbols of Filipino ingenuity, resilience, and the harmonious relationship between people and nature.
